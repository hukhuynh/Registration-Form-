Purpose

The purpose of the Collabowrite is to provide a friendly, creative atmosphere for writers to collaborate on projects together both with illustrations and real time editing. There will also be discussion boards for people to must one another based on similar interests. The site will provide a powerful outlet for writers to create beautiful stories together.

Needs Analysis

  • Provide friendly, safe environment for writers(censorship not enforced but filtered if necessary)
  • Showcase projects to community
  • Discussion boards for common interests
  • FAQ
  • Contact Information

Goals

  1. Provide a safe, friendly, and creative environment for writers to brainstorm their ideas and work on their projects with others.
  2. Provide an online area for people to discuss ideas based on common interests.

Audience Characteristics

People who generally would use Collabowrite would generally be:

  • Poetry
    • Usuallly solo workers, but may need feedback from other users
    • Many different styles of poetry
  • Short
    • These stories may also be solo projects, but may have use of feedback
    • Generally, following a certain theme
  • Novels
    • These will most likely have collaborative writers working on it.
  • Scripts/Screenplays
    • Definitely or most likely requiring collaborative work
  • Casual Writers
    • Most likely working on short projects or working a small bit in huge projects
    • May not have much experience in writing compared to more experienced writers
  • Experienced/Knowledgable writers
    • May spend countless hrs on projects on end
